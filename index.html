About the R8

The Audi R8 debuted in 2007 with a brand new design based on the Le Mans concept car. It features all LED headlights, a mid-car engine, and curves fitting for a supercar.

The Audi R8 has had several engine and gearbox configurations over the years. These include a naturally aspirated 4.2 liter V8 (producing 400+ hp) and 5.2 liter V10 (producing 500-600+ hp). Known for its refined sound, these engines are still regarded as some of the best every created. Most R8 models came with a 6 speed gated manual or a 7 speed automatic.

Depending on the model, the R8 shoots to 60mph somewhere between 3.0 and 4.5 seconds.

The 2007 base R8 cost around $110,000. Newer models have increased in the price and the current price for a 2023 R8 starts at $158,600. Given that 2023 was the last year for R8 production, these prices will likely continue to rise. Currently, 2007 R8s will go for around $60,000.
